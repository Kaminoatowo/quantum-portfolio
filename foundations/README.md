# Foundations of Quantum Computing

---

## Scope (Month 1 – Foundations)

### Theoretical topics

- Hilbert spaces and Dirac notation
- Pure and mixed quantum states
- Unitary operators and quantum gates
- Measurement postulates
- Computational complexity of basic algorithms

### Algorithms implemented

- Deutsch–Jozsa algorithm
- Grover search (small-scale)
- Quantum Phase Estimation (introductory version)
